Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in stealing secrets from deep within the subconscious during dreams. He is offered a chance to erase his criminal past by performing the impossible task of inception—planting an idea into someone’s mind. As dreams layer within dreams, the line between reality and imagination begins to blur.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, supported by a talented cast including Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, Ken Watanabe, and Marion Cotillard. Each actor brings depth and emotion to a complex, multi-layered story.

Favorite Quote

“You mustn’t be afraid to dream a little bigger, darling.” This quote perfectly captures the ambition and imagination that define the movie.
