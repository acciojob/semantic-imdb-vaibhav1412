Inception

A science fiction thriller about dreams within dreams.

Actors Quotes
Inception follows Dom Cobb, a professional thief who steals secrets from people's subconscious minds while they dream. He is given a chance to clear his criminal record by planting an idea into someone’s mind instead of stealing one.

Actors

The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, Marion Cotillard, and Ken Watanabe.
